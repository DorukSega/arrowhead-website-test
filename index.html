What is Arrowhead?
Arrowhead is a Half-Life 2 mod depicting the so-called “First Days,” when Xen flora and fauna, however briefly, ruled the Earth. Set after Half-Life 1 but before the arrival of the decisive Seven Hour War, Arrowhead follows one unassuming survivor’s journey to safety amidst the upheaval of society. Portal Storms have swept the Earth... and they’ve brought vicious, hungry creatures from another world. Enter the Half-Life universe and experience the public’s first unguarded struggle against the flora and fauna of Xen. Just remember to stay alert.

When you least expect it, there’s always something lurking in the dark.

FAQ
So what’s Arrowhead about?
Arrowhead follows an unnamed survivor as they travel across contemporary locations which have been quickly overrun by Xen creatures, some acting intelligently and others driven by pure, animalistic instinct. It’s our goal to tell a fun and unique story in the Half-Life universe, making creative use of existing story elements while adding plenty of our own flavour and inspirations to the mix. We hope you’ll enjoy it!
Will it be free?
We can’t legally sell it, and unless we get the miraculous legal ability to do so, it’ll be free. It’s a mod, so you’ll definitely need Half-Life 2 to play it, but you probably already knew that!
Will it be released on Steam?
Maybe! Hopefully! We want to. It’s too early to say how much extra work that’ll be for us. There are plenty of other options, like ModDB, so the game will be easy to distribute regardless.
How do I join the team?
There’s a link to apply with more information about the process below! It’s as simple as filling in a form to the best of your ability and letting us do the rest.
What can I apply for?
We typically make a small number of positions available to apply for at a time so we can guarantee that applicants will hear back from us and won’t be waiting speculatively for too long. We only ask for roles when we know we can give those people proper direction, so if you don’t see something you want on the list, there’s a chance we just aren’t ready for you yet! Check the application to see what’s available. If it isn’t what you want, keep an eye on our Twitter for new roles as they become available.
When will it be done?
There is no reasonable way to estimate this—including giving you a percentage to completion, which is impossible—but expect to wait more than a few years before the full game is complete. We’re a team of unpaid volunteers working whenever we can, and we’re trying to have fun, too. That means no deadlines and nothing that would speed up development but lead to inevitable burnout. Still, we won’t keep people in the dark about our progress! We hope you all enjoy following the development of the game as much as we enjoy working on it.
Will we see [x alien/weapon/location here]?
There are ideas for lots of things we’re sure people would love to see, but we generally don’t announce things that’ll be in the game unless we have reasonable confidence that the information won’t age poorly and that we’ll have something to show for it. Feel free to ask, but don’t be surprised if we don’t comment!
How can I follow the project?
Our Twitter is the best place to keep up on news about Arrowhead and Arrowhead adjacent stuff. If you’d like a more unfiltered look into the development and conceptualization of things, follow our team members on Twitter! There’s a huge listing of them all below. Check them out!

Join Us!
We’re a small team of volunteers who are always excited to work with like-minded people! We avoid things like deadlines or a required amount of weekly contributions, and just try to foster a fun, creative space for fans and developers to get together and make a neat thing. If you want a stress-free and collaborative environment where designers, artists, and programmers come together to make cool stuff, consider joining! Check the application to see what roles are available. If none of them are for you, we might not need you yet. We announce new roles as they become available, so keep an eye out!
